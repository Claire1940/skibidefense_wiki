<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Strategy & Wave Management">

The defining mechanic of **skibi defense chapter 9** is the spawn point relocation. At Wave 10, the direction of the enemy attack changes entirely, forcing you to pivot your entire defensive setup.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Establish Early Economy and Defense">
    Begin by placing Bladerunner Speakerguys and Brute Speakerguys near the first spawn point to handle the initial waves. Simultaneously, place Dancing Cameras in a safe corner and begin upgrading them immediately to build your cash reserves. Use Upgraded DJ Girl to buff your defenders.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Prepare the Secondary Spawn Point">
    Before Wave 10 begins, place Alliance DJ at the secondary spawn point. Do not upgrade it yet, but ensure it is positioned to cover the path where the new wave of enemies will emerge. Keep farming with your Dancing Cameras.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Wave 10 Pivot">
    As soon as Wave 10 starts, the spawn points switch. Immediately sell all defense units at the initial spawn point. Relocate your defensive line to the Alliance DJ. Place Upgraded DJ Girl and Upgraded Polycephaly (equipped with the Konda perk and Enhanced Armor skin) and max them out.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Deploy Titans for the Destructor Boss">
    Place down the Upgraded DJ Toilet Mech and begin upgrading him. As the final boss, the Destructor, approaches, deploy your Prime Colossal Camera or Upgraded Colossal TV Guy to deliver the final blow. If needed, use G-Toilet 4.0 to speed up the process.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Managing this transition smoothly is the difference between an easy victory and a sudden defeat. Use the table below to track your actions per wave.

| Wave Range | Primary Focus | Key Action |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Waves 1-9 | Economy & Base Defense | Maximize Dancing Cameras; hold the line with Brutes. |
| Wave 10 | Relocation | Sell starting units; place Upgraded Polycephaly at spawn 2. |
| Waves 11-19 | Mid-Game Scaling | Upgrade DJ Toilet Mech; max out support buffs. |
| Wave 20 | Boss Elimination | Deploy Prime Colossal Camera; focus fire on the Destructor. |

<Callout type="warning" title="Selling Speed">
Do not hesitate during the Wave 10 transition. If you delay selling your early-game units, you will lack the funds to deploy Upgraded Polycephaly at the second spawn point, leading to an immediate leak.
</Callout>
