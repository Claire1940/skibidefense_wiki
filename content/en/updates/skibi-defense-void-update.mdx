<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Jeffrey, Ebony of the Void: Stats & Abilities">

Unlocking **Jeffrey, Ebony of the Void** rewards you with one of the most powerful and visually striking towers in the game. Jeffrey is a colossal unit that dominates the battlefield, but using him effectively requires understanding his unique, double-edged mechanics.

Jeffrey's primary gimmick is his ability to absorb power from other towers. When placed, he takes a set value from all other active towers on the map as a negative damage boost and adds it directly to his own damage. This effect stacks infinitely, making him exceptionally strong when paired with high-value units like TV variants.

| Ability / Attack Name | Damage Type | Base DPS | Special Mechanical Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Void Claws** | Melee Slash | 100 Million | Fast, single-target physical damage |
| **Arm Slam** | AoE Smash | 200 Million | Heavy ground slam with splash damage |
| **Void Screamer** | Global Buff | N/A | Grants all towers a 6x damage buff for 60 seconds |
| **Dark Haste** | Fire Rate Buff | N/A | Grants a temporary 3% fire rate increase to all units |

However, Jeffrey's active abilities carry severe penalties once their durations expire. For example, after the 6x damage buff from **Void Screamer** ends, all affected towers receive a permanent -1% damage penalty. Additionally, his active abilities share a global cooldown and lock permanently after a set number of uses.

<Callout type="success" title="Ideal Jeffrey Synergy">
Because Jeffrey drains power from other placed towers to boost his own stats, pair him with cheap, high-value TV units. This maximizes his passive damage multiplier, which can push his total DPS past 830 million.
</Callout>
