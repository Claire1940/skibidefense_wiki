  <Step num="2" title="Mid-Wave Stabilization">

  <Step num="3" title="Titan Ascension">

  <Step num="4" title="The Standoff & Retreat">

<Section icon={Shield} title="Final Boss Analysis: Obliterator Astro">

The Obliterator Astro is the ultimate test in **skibi defense chapter 8**. As a member of the Astros Faction, it possesses unique movement patterns and high resistance to basic stuns.

**Boss Statistics:**
- **Health**: 5,250,000,000 (5.25B)
- **Faction**: Astros
- **Special Mechanics**: Immense health pool designed to tank base damage.

**Counter Tactics:**
1. **Knockback**: Use units with sound blast or knockback abilities to reset the boss's position.
2. **Time Manipulation**: Clock Woman is non-negotiable for her ability to slow enemies and speed up your heavy hitters.
3. **Stun Prevention**: The boss and its minions will frequently stun your towers. Place 3 maxed Medic Cameramen in a triangle formation to ensure coverage for your main DPS units.

| Boss Phase | HP Threshold | Recommended Action |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Initial Spawn** | 5.25B | Activate all boosts immediately |
| **Mid-Fight** | 2.5B | Check Medic coverage; re-apply boosts |
| **Final Stretch** | 500M | Activate Rage Mode and Manual Control |

<Callout type="warning" title="Base Tanking">
While it is technically possible to "tank" the final boss with base health, it is not recommended for a reliable win. Focus on dealing damage rather than relying on base durability.
</Callout>
