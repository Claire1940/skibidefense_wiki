To access the high-tier units in the **skibi defense armada update**, you cannot simply spend gems. You must prove your worth through the Sovereign of Armada questline. This is a linear progression path where each unit's quest must be completed and claimed before the next unit becomes available for unlocking.

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Progress to Brute & Support">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Elite Unit Acquisition">

  <Step num="4" title="The Titan Trials">

<Section icon={Zap} title="Advanced Mechanics: Execution and Control">

One of the standout features of the **skibi defense armada update** is the introduction of manual control and the "Execution" keyword. These mechanics allow for a level of player agency previously unseen in Skibi Defense.

### Manual Cockpit Control
Units like the **Upgraded Speaker Strider** and the **Executor** allow players to enter a first-person or close-third-person "Cockpit View." 
- **Strider**: Can perform backflips and jetpack maneuvers to reposition or dodge boss attacks while unleashing sonic shrieks.
- **Executor**: Players can manually trigger the "Scythe Throw" and "Stomp" abilities to time them with enemy wave clusters.

### The Execution Mechanic
The Executor unit introduces a game-changing ability that can bypass massive boss health pools:
1. **Highlighting**: The first click of the ability highlights all enemies on the map with 15% or less HP.
2. **Instant Kill**: The second click instantly kills every highlighted enemy, regardless of their total health or shield status.

<Callout type="info" title="Boss Strategy">
The Execution ability is perfect for Nightmare mode bosses. If a boss has 100 billion HP, the Executor can instantly delete the final 15 billion HP with a single click once the threshold is met.
</Callout>

</Section>
