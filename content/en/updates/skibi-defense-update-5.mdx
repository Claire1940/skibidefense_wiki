Navigating the newly updated maps in **skibi defense update 5** requires a deep understanding of enemy pathing and wave compositions. Each map presents unique environmental challenges, varying path lengths, and specific choke points where you should concentrate your defensive forces.

The update has adjusted the wave scaling, meaning enemies gain health and speed much faster than in previous versions. | Map Name | Difficulty | Enemy Pattern | Recommended Strategy |

| Mid-Game Map | Normal | Mixed ground and ranged enemies | Balanced tower placement with AoE support |
| Advanced Map | Hard | High HP enemies with armored units | High DPS single-target towers and stun effects |
| Endgame Map | Insane | Fast boss waves and elite enemies | Full optimized loadout with synergy-based tower combinations |

To consistently clear the hardest content, players must follow a structured deployment plan. Spreading your resources too thin across the map is a common mistake that leads to early defeats.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Establish Early Economy">
    Deploy low-cost units like the basic Cameraman at key choke points to handle early, fast-moving waves. Avoid over-upgrading early units to save resources.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Transition to Mid-Game AoE">
    Place Large TV Man or Large Cameraman near corners to maximize their area-of-effect damage. This ensures larger groups of enemies are cleared before reaching the exit.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Deploy Late-Game Titans">
    As the waves progress, save up coins to deploy Titan Cameraman or Titan TV Man. Position them where they can cover multiple paths or long straight lines.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Maximize Titan Upgrades">
    Focus your resources on fully upgrading your Titan units. A single max-level Titan is far more effective than multiple low-level units during intense boss waves.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Choke Point Placement">
Never crowd your units at the very beginning of the path. Instead, focus your defenses around loops and intersections to maximize the duration enemies stay in range.
</Callout>

By concentrating your damage dealers around corners, you ensure that splash damage hits the maximum number of targets. Always keep an eye on the wave counter to prepare for upcoming boss fights.
