<Section icon={Shield} title="Defeating the Final Boss: Astro Jugg">

The final boss of **skibi defense chapter 7** is the Astro Jugg. Boasting a staggering 10 Qi health, this boss requires sustained damage and tactical ability usage. Unlike previous chapters, you cannot rely solely on raw power; you must use knockback mechanics effectively.

| Boss Attribute | Value | Strategy |
|----------------|-------|----------|
| Total Health | 10 Qi | Sustained DPS / Buffs |
| Movement Speed | Moderate | Use Sound Blast Knockback |
| Special Ability | Stun Wave | Counter with Max Medics |
| Weakness | Overlapping Buffs | Stack DJ and Speaker boosts |

To defeat the Astro Jugg, wait until it reaches the midpoint of the map. Use the Sound Blast ability to push back any secondary bosses (like G-Toilet) so that your Titan Camera Toilet can focus entirely on the Juggernaut. Because the Juggernaut must reach the base to trigger the final win condition, your goal is to whittle its health down as it approaches.

<Callout type="info" title="Boss Mechanic">
The Astro Jugg has high resistance to certain slow effects. Prioritize raw damage output and knockback over freezing or slowing units for this specific encounter.
</Callout>
