<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Unit Progression Strategy">

Navigating the progression path in Skibi Defense requires careful planning. Wasting credits on mediocre early-game towers will delay your access to late-game powerhouses like Juggernaut. Follow this step-by-step strategy to optimize your credit spending and unit acquisition.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Secure Early Game Survival">
    Start by purchasing **Normal Camera Guy** and **Camera Reaper**. These units are cheap, highly effective for the first few chapters, and will help you clear basic stages to accumulate your first few hundred thousand credits.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Acquire Mid-Game Carries">
    Save your credits to purchase **Fred** or **Mutant**. Fred is highly recommended because you can acquire him early without needing to beat advanced chapters. These units will easily carry you through Chapters 4, 5, and 6.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Establish Your Economy">
    Unlock **Dancing Camera** and **Alliance DJ Girl**. Practice maximizing your economy during the first 10 waves of any match. This ensures you have the financial backing to support expensive late-game towers.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Clear Chapter 7 & Unlock Juggernaut">
    Use your mid-game setup along with **Medic** to clear Chapter 7. Once cleared, purchase **Juggernaut** for 1.5 million credits. This unlocks your first true late-game DPS tower capable of hitting ~100M DPS.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Grind for Endgame Goated Units">
    With a stable setup, transition to grinding the **Nightmare Raid** for the 1% **Awakened Creator** drop, or save tokens for **Upgraded Titan Clockman** and **Prime Titan Cameraman Colossal**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Progression Milestone">
Do not rush into Nightmare Raids without at least one high-tier DPS like Juggernaut or Gojo and a maxed-out Medic. Doing so will result in failed runs and wasted time.
</Callout>

</Section>
