<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Maximizing Resources and Upgrades">

To progress quickly in Skibi Defense, you must manage your currencies and upgrade items effectively. Credits are the primary currency for units and slots, while tokens, shards, and versus coins unlock premium rewards and silver towers.

Opening crates using tokens is highly recommended. Crates cost five tokens each and have a chance to drop normal or premium upgrade coupons. Premium upgrade coupons are incredibly valuable as they allow you to instantly bypass expensive upgrade levels on your high-DPS units, saving thousands of credits during a match.
