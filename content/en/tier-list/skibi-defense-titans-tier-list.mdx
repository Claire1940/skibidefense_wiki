Titan TV Man is the premier area-of-effect damage dealer. His screen projection attacks melt grouped enemies instantly, making him the ultimate solution for fast-moving swarms and armored waves. If your defense struggles against single-target bosses, Titan Cameraman is the definitive answer. Armed with high-impact physical attacks, this unit delivers devastating blows to high-HP targets. While his area coverage is smaller than Titan TV Man, his raw single-target DPS is unmatched, making him vital for boss waves.

<Section icon={Shield} title="A-Tier and B-Tier Units: Mid-Game Stability">

While S-tier Titans are the ultimate goal, you cannot ignore the units that get you there. A-Tier and B-Tier units provide the necessary stability during early and mid-game waves, allowing you to save resources for your heavy hitters.

### Mid-Game Bridge Units
Large TV Man and Large Cameraman serve as excellent bridge units. Large TV Man offers moderate area control, slowing down fast-moving enemies so your primary damage dealers can clean them up. Large Cameraman provides robust physical damage at a fraction of the cost of a full Titan, making him highly efficient for holding the line during waves 15 to 30.

### The Role of Starter Units
The standard Cameraman is your basic tool for survival. With an incredibly low placement cost, this unit ensures you do not take early damage from weak, fast enemies. However, do not spend too much currency upgrading B-tier units, as their damage scaling is insufficient for late-game threats.
