<Section icon={Swords} title="How to Evaluate Signs and Secrets">

Developer signs add a layer of complexity to **skibi defense values**. A sign is essentially a signature from a developer or a high-profile staff member, and its value is multiplicative based on the unit it is applied to.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Check the Base Unit">
    A sign is only worth its maximum potential if applied to a Diamond or Cursed Secret unit. A sign on a common unit adds negligible value.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Identify the Signer">
    Signs from lead developers like AloXlo or Zeronami carry significantly more weight than junior staff signs.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Verify Authenticity">
    Always check the unit's metadata in the trade window to ensure the sign is legitimate and not a cleverly named imitation.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Consult the O/C Status">
    "Owner's Choice" (O/C) applies when only one instance of a specific signed unit exists. In these cases, the owner sets the price entirely.
  </Step>
</Steps>
