  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Activity} title="Technical Optimization and Lag Reduction">

Nightmare Mode often features hundreds of units and projectiles on screen simultaneously. This can lead to severe frame rate drops or game crashes, especially during the Land of Creation waves.

**Video Evidence:** Even high-end PCs can struggle with Nightmare Mode. During the final waves, the frame rate can drop to 1 FPS if settings are not adjusted.

<Callout type="warning" title="Performance Alert">
If your game freezes, do not immediately Alt+F4. Try to open the settings menu and toggle the following options to "Disabled" or "Low" to recover your frame rate.
</Callout>

| Setting | Recommended Value | Impact on Gameplay |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Particles** | Disabled | High - Reduces GPU load significantly |
| **Projectiles** | Disabled | High - Clears visual clutter |
| **Unit Animations** | Off | Medium - Helps with CPU overhead |
| **Damage Indicators** | Off | Medium - Stops text-spam lag |
| **Quality Level** | Low | General performance boost |

</Section>
