<Section icon={Target} title="Wave-by-Wave Progression Strategy">

Success in Endless Mode requires a structured approach. You cannot simply place units randomly and expect to survive. Following a step-by-step progression plan ensures your economy grows fast enough to afford expensive upgrades before the scaling outpaces your damage output.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Early Game Setup (Waves 1-40)">
    Focus entirely on placing your economy units first. Place a cheap defender at the start of the track to handle early waves while you max out your farms. Do not over-spend on defensive units during this phase.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Mid Game Transition (Waves 41-100)">
    Begin placing mid-tier DPS units near the front of the track. Upgrade your defense gradually as enemy health pools increase. Ensure you have at least one slow unit active to manage fast-moving waves.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Late Game Optimization (Waves 101-180)">
    Start replacing your early-game defenders with high-tier DPS units like Diamond Gojo. Maximize the upgrades on your primary damage dealers. Place support units nearby to buff attack speeds.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Endless Push (Waves 181+)">
    Sell some of your farms to free up unit space if you have reached the placement limit. Fill the extra slots with additional support or high-damage units. Keep a close eye on boss waves to activate manual abilities.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Advanced Strategy & Placement Tips">

Placement is critical when trying to survive past Wave 180. Placing units at corners allows them to maximize their range and attack enemies for a longer duration. Additionally, overlapping your support unit ranges ensures that your DPS units receive continuous buffs without any downtime.

Make sure to leave space near the front of the track for slow and stun units. This delays enemies in the kill zone of your main DPS units, allowing your area-of-effect attacks to hit as many targets as possible.
