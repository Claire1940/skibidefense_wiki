Understanding the economy is the first step in this **skibi defense beginner guide**. You will encounter several types of currency, each serving a specific purpose in your progression. Credits are your primary tool for purchasing units and expanding your loadout slots. While Premium Coins exist as a pay-to-win currency, diligent players can obtain them for free through gameplay, though they should be saved for high-value game passes like the "3x Speed" modifier.

| Currency Type | Primary Acquisition | Best Use Case | Priority |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Credits** | Playing Matches / Codes | Buying Units / Loadout Slots | **High** |
| **Shards** | Trials / Late Game Acts | Upgrading Specific Units | **Medium** |
| **Versus Coins** | Versus Mode | Silver Tower Units | **Medium** |
| **Premium Coins** | Daily Rewards / Robux | Game Modifiers / Luck Boosts | **Low** |
| **Tokens** | Special Tasks | Opening Resource Crates | **High** |

Your loadout is the foundation of your strategy. In Skibi Defense, you start with five slots, but you can purchase more using Credits. A common mistake for beginners is filling their loadout with only high-damage units. Without a balanced team, you will likely run out of funds before you can place your heavy hitters.

<Section icon={Target} title="Progression Steps for New Players">

Following a structured path will help you reach Chapter 7 and beyond much faster. This **skibi defense beginner guide** recommends focusing on Act 1 until you have a solid grasp of your unit's ranges and abilities.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Resource Gathering">
    Start by playing Act 1 and redeeming all available codes. Use your initial credits to buy a reliable early-game unit and your first "Money Maker" unit.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Unlocking Loadout Slots">
    As soon as you have a surplus of credits, buy your 6th and 7th loadout slots. Having more variety in your team allows you to handle different enemy types, such as fast or armored units.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Crate and Coupon Farming">
    Spend your tokens on the 5-token crates. These frequently drop Premium Upgrade Coupons, which are vital for leveling up expensive Titan units without spending in-game cash during a match.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Shard Optimization">
    Once you reach the mid-game (Chapters 7-9), start focusing on Shards. Prioritize Diamond and Mega Diamond shards, as Golden shards offer diminishing returns for the effort required.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Daily Routine">
Don't forget to claim your daily rewards every 24 hours. These often include Premium Coins and Boosts that significantly reduce the grind.
</Callout>

</Section>

Once you are comfortable with the basics, you should start using game modifiers to increase your Credit and XP yields. However, you must choose your modifiers wisely to avoid making the game impossible or unnecessarily long.

| Modifier Name | Effect | Reward Bonus | Recommendation |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Powerful Enemies** | Enemies have more HP | +50% Credits | **Recommended** |
| **Double Enemies** | Twice the enemy count | +100% Credits | **Avoid (Too Slow)** |
| **Fast Enemies** | Enemies move quicker | +25% Credits | **Use with Slowers** |
