<Section icon={Shield} title="Boss Counter and Tactical Adjustments">

The final boss of Chapter 6 is notorious for its "Stun Scream" ability, which can temporarily disable your towers. To counter this, you must stagger your unit placement. Do not clump all your Titans in one spot; spread them out so that if one is stunned, others can continue firing.

The boss also possesses a massive HP pool that regenerates if it isn't hit for several seconds. Constant damage is mandatory. This is where the **Titan Cameraman**'s high fire rate becomes essential.

| Boss Ability | Effect | Best Counter Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Stun Scream** | Disables units for 3s | Spread units across the map |
| **Speed Boost** | Increases movement by 50% | Use **Large TV Man** for constant stuns |
| **Armor Plating** | Reduces physical damage | Use energy-based units or Titans |
| **Summon Minions** | Spawns small toilets | Ensure **Titan TV Man** is covering the path |

<Callout type="warning" title="Warning">
If the boss reaches the final turn with more than 20% HP, activate all unit special abilities immediately. This is your last window for a successful clear.
</Callout>
