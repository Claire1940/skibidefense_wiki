<Section icon={Shield} title="Understanding Crates and Drop Rates">

Crates serve as the primary progression mechanic in Skibi Defense. To build a powerful army of defenders, you must understand how to acquire and open these containers. They contain essential units from various factions, including the Camera Faction, Speaker Faction, TV Faction, and the elusive Astros Faction. Opening crates allows you to recruit powerful characters such as the Camera Fighter, Speaker Fighter, and even mythic-tier defenders like the TV Titan.

Each crate type has a distinct cost structure and drop rate configuration. Knowing which crate to target prevents you from wasting valuable credits on units that do not fit your defensive strategy.

| Crate Type | Cost (Credits) | Key Drops | Drop Rates |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |

| **Elite Crate** | 500 | Elite Mercenary, Camera Strider | Rare: **60%**, Epic: **35%**, Legendary: **5%** |

<Section icon={Map} title="Step-by-Step Guide to Purchasing Crates">

Once you have gathered enough credits, navigating the interface to purchase your crates is straightforward. Follow these steps to access the shop and claim your rewards.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Access the Summon Shop">
    Navigate to the main lobby area of Skibi Defense. Locate the Summon Shop zone or click the Shop icon located on the left-hand side of your screen.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Select Your Crate Category">
    Browse through the available crate options. Ensure you select the crate that corresponds to your target faction or rarity tier.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Confirm Your Purchase">
    Click on the purchase button. A confirmation prompt will appear showing the credit deduction. Confirm the transaction to proceed.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Open and Equip Your Units">
    Watch the opening animation to reveal your new unit. Go to your inventory screen to equip the unit into your active loadout before starting your next match.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Inventory Warning">
Ensure you have open slots in your character inventory before purchasing multiple crates. If your inventory is full, you cannot open new crates until you delete or merge existing units.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Maximizing Your Crate Farming Efficiency">

To get crates as fast as possible, you must optimize your credit-per-minute ratio. Speedrunning specific maps on higher difficulties is much faster than playing long, drawn-out matches on lower difficulties. Using towers with high Area of Effect (AoE) damage allows you to clear early waves quickly, triggering the wave skip feature to accelerate your completion times.

Playing with friends or experienced players in multiplayer lobbies also grants a cooperative play bonus, which increases your overall credit payout at the end of the match.

| Map Name | Recommended Difficulty | Core Strategy | Average Clear Time | Credit Yield |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |

| **Desert Outpost** | Hard | Slow units + high single-target DPS | **15 Minutes** | **220 Credits** |
| **Toy Factory** | Nightmare | Full TV Faction support setup | **20 Minutes** | **350 Credits** |
