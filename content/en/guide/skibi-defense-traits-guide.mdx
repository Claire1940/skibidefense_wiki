<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Understanding the Traits System in Skibi Defense">

In the high-stakes environment of Skibi Defense, simply deploying a high-rarity unit isn't enough to survive the Insane difficulty maps or infinite waves. Traits act as the definitive X-factor, providing percentage-based or flat-rate bonuses to your towers. These modifiers are assigned randomly when a unit is summoned or can be changed through the reroll mechanic found in the main lobby. Understanding how these traits interact with unit roles—such as AOE, Single Target, or Support—is the difference between a wave 30 defeat and a wave 100 victory.

| Trait Category | Primary Bonus | Impact Level | Best Unit Type |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Offensive** | Damage, Crit Chance | High | Main DPS (Titans) |
| **Speed** | Attack Cooldown | Medium | Stun/Slow Units |
| **Utility** | Range, Placement Cost | High | Support/Area Control |
| **Economic** | Wave Income, Sell Value | Essential | Early Game Starters |

<Section icon={Zap} title="How to Reroll Traits: A Step-by-Step Guide">

Rerolling is the primary way to optimize your team. It requires Reroll Tokens, which can be obtained through daily quests, codes, or the in-game shop. Follow these steps to ensure you don't waste your resources on the wrong units.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Access the Trait Lab">
    Navigate to the main lobby and locate the Trait Lab NPC. Ensure you have the unit you wish to modify equipped in your current loadout.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Select Your Unit">
    Choose the unit from your inventory. The UI will display the current trait and the potential stat changes if you roll a higher rarity modifier.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Check Token Balance">
    Verify you have enough Reroll Tokens. Legendary and Mythic units often require more tokens per roll or specialized "Super Rerolls" to guarantee better results.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Confirm the Roll">
    Click the reroll button. Note that you cannot "lock" traits in the standard lab; once you roll, the old trait is replaced immediately unless you use a "Trait Lock" item.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Note">
The probability of hitting a Mythic trait like **Godly** is approximately 0.1% to 0.5%. It is recommended to save your tokens for Legendary units only, as Common or Rare units are easily replaced as you progress.
</Callout>

</Section>
