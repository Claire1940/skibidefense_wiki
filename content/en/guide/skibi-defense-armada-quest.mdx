<Section icon={Swords} title="Completing the Skibi Defense Armada Quest for Bladerunner">

The first major hurdle in the **skibi defense armada quest** series is the Bladerunner Speakerguy's quest, also known as **Power the Armada**. This phase is highly combat-intensive, requiring you to deal a massive amount of cumulative damage and hunt down specific controlled Speaker variants that only spawn in particular chapters.

To farm the 150 Controlled Speakers and 60 Large Controlled Speakers efficiently, focus your efforts on Chapter 4. This map features a high density of controlled speaker enemies, allowing you to progress multiple objectives simultaneously. For the colossal variants, you must defeat the Controlled Speaker Colossal 6 times and its upgraded version 3 times.

| Objective | Target Requirement | Recommended Strategy |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Kill Enemies | 2500 Targets | Run Endless Mode with auto-skip enabled. |
| Win Chapter 1 | 1 Time | Equip Speakerguy and Large Speakerguy in loadout. |
| Win Chapter 4 | 3 Times | Keep Dark Speakerguy in your active loadout. |
| Upgrade Towers | 50 Times | Fully upgrade any non-event Speaker tower in-game. |
| Defeat Controlled | 150 Speakers | Grind Chapter 4 on Normal or Hard difficulty. |
| Defeat Colossal | 6 Colossals / 3 Upgraded | Target boss waves in Chapter 4 and Chapter 5. |
| Deal Damage | 1,000,000,000 Damage | Use high-DPS Speaker units in late Endless waves. |

Completing this quest awards you the Bladerunner Speakerguy, 100,000 credits, and unlocks the next phase of the questline. Bladerunner Speakerguy is an excellent early-to-mid-game unit with wide slash attacks and high single-target DPS, making him a valuable asset for subsequent challenges.

<Callout type="warning" title="Tracking Warning">
Make sure you do not use event or exclusive Speaker towers for the damage and upgrade objectives. Only standard, non-event Speaker towers will progress the 1 billion damage counter.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Target} title="Brute Speakerguy Quest: Armada's Crusher">

Once you unlock Bladerunner, you immediately gain access to the **Armada's Crusher** questline to obtain the Brute Speakerguy. This phase shifts the focus toward boss elimination and specific chapter mastery. You will need to defeat G-Man 3.0 multiple times and secure wins on Chapters 4 and 5 with highly specific loadouts.

G-Man 3.0 can be a tough boss to bring down if your defense lacks slow or stun mechanics. Utilizing support units like Timer Clockman or DJ Girl can provide the utility needed to keep G-Man in the attack range of your primary damage dealers.

| Objective | Target | Best Farm Method |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Kill Toilets | 5000 Toilets | Use standard Speaker towers in Chapter 3 or 4. |
| Win Chapter 4 | 3 Times | Must have Upgraded Speakerguy equipped in loadout. |
| Win Chapter 5 | 3 Times | Must have Upgraded Speaker Colossal equipped. |
| Defeat Bosses | 7 G-Man 3.0 | Farm Chapter 4 where G-Man spawns reliably. |

To optimize this grind, combine your Chapter 4 runs with your G-Man hunting. Since Chapter 4 naturally features G-Man encounters, completing your three required wins there will simultaneously knock out a significant portion of your boss elimination target.

<Callout type="success" title="Loadout Synergy">
Pairing Upgraded Speaker Colossal with DJ Girl ensures your colossal units receive a massive range and attack speed buff, making the Chapter 5 clears significantly easier.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Upgraded Large Speakerguy Quest & Chapter 8 Walkthrough">

The third phase of the **skibi defense armada quest** introduces highly specific enemy targets, such as Astro Toilets and Mafia Toilets, alongside a mandatory victory on Chapter 8. Chapter 8 is notoriously difficult due to its compact map layout and fast-paced enemy paths, requiring precise placement and rapid economy scaling.

| Objective | Target Count | Key Strategy |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Win Chapter 1.5 | 1 Time | Equip Large Speaker and Speaker Colossal. |
| Win Chapter 8 | 1 Time | Use a high-DPS, compact layout near the exit. |
| Defeat Astros | 100 Astro Toilets | Play Chapters 7 and 8 where Astros spawn frequently. |
| Defeat Toilets | 1200 with Large Speaker | Place Large Speaker at the start of Endless Mode. |
| Defeat Buff Mutants | 3 Large Buff Mutants | Focus fire on boss waves in Chapters 7 and 8. |
| Defeat Mafia Toilets | 50 Total / 3 with Large Speaker | Use Large Speaker to land the final blow on Mafia units. |

To help you conquer the toughest part of this phase, follow this tactical step-by-step setup for Chapter 8.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Early Economy Setup">
    Begin by placing Upgraded Silver or a similar low-cost defense unit near the central loop. Immediately start placing and upgrading your money farms to scale your income before the fast-moving Astro waves arrive.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Crowd Control Deployment">
    Deploy DJ Girl in a central location to buff your surrounding towers. Place a Timer Clockman or another slowing unit near the entrance of the main loop to stall high-speed toilets.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Colossal Placement">
    As your income maximizes, deploy your Upgraded Speaker Colossal or other heavy damage dealers. Position them so their attack radius covers both the entrance and exit paths of the loop to maximize their damage uptime.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Map Danger">
Chapter 8 features extremely tight corridors. Avoid placing large-footprint units in areas where they block optimal placement spots for your support towers. Plan your grid before placing heavy units.
</Callout>
