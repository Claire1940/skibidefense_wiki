<Section icon={Shield} title="Wave 50 Boss Tactics & Defense Optimization">

The final confrontation in Chapter 8 pits your defense against the Giant Toilet Boss. This massive threat boasts millions of health points and possesses a passive ability that temporarily disables nearby defensive towers. Relying solely on raw damage will not work; you must utilize placement depth and stun-stalling tactics.

To counter the boss's disabling scream, space your high-tier units out along the path rather than clustering them in a single spot. This ensures that when one group of titans is stunned, another group further down the line can continue firing.

| Boss Ability | Effect on Towers | Defensive Counter |
| Stun Laser | Disables units for 5 seconds | Spread out high-range units |
| Speed Boost | Doubles movement speed | Chain slow abilities and stuns |
| Summon Minions | Spawns shielding scouts | Position secondary AoE near exit |

When the boss enters the final stretch of the track, players should prepare to micro-manage their towers. Selling units that the boss has already walked past and rebuilding them further ahead near the exit is a highly effective method to maximize your damage output.

<Callout type="success" title="Victory Condition">
Maintaining at least three active, non-stunned Titan units at all times is the key to defeating the boss. Use your slowing units to keep the boss within the firing arc of your maxed-out Titan Cameramen for as long as possible.
