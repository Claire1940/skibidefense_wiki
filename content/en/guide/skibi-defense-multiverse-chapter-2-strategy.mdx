Executing the perfect placement pattern is critical to surviving the escalating difficulty of Multiverse Chapter 2. This step-by-step walkthrough details how to transition your defense from the early-game economy setup to the late-game Titan onslaught.

  <Step num="1" title="Early Economy Setup (Waves 1-10)">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Mid-Game Choke Point Control (Waves 11-30)">
    As armored units begin to spawn, deploy a Large TV Man at the front of the central intersection. Upgrade this unit to level 3 as quickly as possible to unlock its area-of-effect slow ability. Place a Large Cameraman slightly behind it to clean up any units that manage to slip past the main stun field.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Late-Game Titan Deployment (Waves 31-45)">
    Save your coins to deploy your first Legendary Titan. Place the Titan TV Man in the center of the loop, ensuring its massive range covers both the incoming lanes and the central choke point. Once deployed, focus all your resources on upgrading its damage output to handle the incoming Giant Mutants.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Ultimate Boss Showdown (Waves 46-50)">

  </Step>
</Steps>
