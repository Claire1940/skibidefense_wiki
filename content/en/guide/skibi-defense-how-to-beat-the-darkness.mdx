<Section icon={Map} title="Darkness Map Mechanics & Wave Structure">

The Darkness map is one of the most demanding challenges in Skibi Defense. Unlike standard maps, this environment introduces a persistent fog modifier that actively limits your defensive capabilities. Successfully navigating this stage requires a deep understanding of enemy pathing, wave compositions, and how the environmental debuff affects your unit placement.

The defining mechanic of this map is the **Darkness Fog**. This modifier reduces the base attack range of all deployed towers by 20%. This penalty makes narrow choke points and overlapping fields of fire incredibly important. Additionally, enemies spawn at a faster rate, meaning single-target towers can easily become overwhelmed if you do not mix in sufficient area-of-effect (AoE) damage.

| Wave | Key Enemies | Danger Level | Recommended Counter |
|------|-------------|--------------|---------------------|

| Wave 25 | Armored Dark Toilets | High | Titan Cameraman Single Target |
| Wave 40 | Elite Void Walkers | Very High | Stun and Slow Units |
| Wave 50 | The Dark Titan Boss | Extreme | Maxed Titan TV Man DPS |

To survive the late-game pushes, you must identify the map's primary intersection points. Placing your high-damage units at the central loops ensures they can strike enemies multiple times along the winding path, compensating for the range reduction.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Boss Fight Strategy: Defeating the Dark Titan">

The final hurdle on this map is the Dark Titan, a massive boss unit that spawns on Wave 50. Boasting an immense health pool and the ability to temporarily disable your towers with a shadow scream, this boss requires active management and precise unit targeting.

The Dark Titan moves slowly but possesses three distinct phases. In each phase, he gains new abilities that can disrupt your defensive line. Understanding these phases and preparing the correct counters is the key to surviving the final wave.

| Boss Phase | Key Threat | Strategic Counter |
|------------|------------|-------------------|
| Phase 1: Shielded | High damage shield | Deploy high attack speed units |
| Phase 2: Rage Mode | Stun screams | Place stun-resistant support towers |
| Phase 3: Void Blast | Lane-wide wipe | Cycle backup DPS units instantly |

During Phase 2, the boss will periodically emit a scream that disables adjacent towers for 5 seconds. To counter this, space out your Titan TV Men so that they are not all caught in the same area-of-effect stun. This ensures that while one group of towers is disabled, another group further down the track can continue dealing damage.

<Callout type="info" title="Tactical Placement">
Keep a pool of reserve gold during Wave 50. If the Dark Titan manages to push past your primary defensive loop, sell your rear units and instantly redeploy them further down the track to get extra damage output before he reaches the exit.
</Callout>
