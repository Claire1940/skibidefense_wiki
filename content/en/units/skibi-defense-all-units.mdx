### Pencil
The standard Pencil is your introductory cloning unit. It features a low placement limit of eight and serves primarily to clone cheaper units during the early waves. While its base DPS is low, its ability to duplicate key assets makes it highly versatile for budget setups.

### Pencil Helicopter
This unit acts as a summoner that deploys mini pens to fight on the front lines. It features a line attack pattern that helps thin out packed groups of enemies. It does not possess cloning capabilities, focusing instead on continuous unit spawning.

### Large Hammer Pencil
A short-range brawler that deals high damage with a low cooldown. It features a 10-meter line AoE width, making it excellent for bottleneck points on the map. Its maximum DPS reaches 369,000, making it a reliable mid-game defender.

### Large Pencil Summoner
A powerful upgrade over the helicopter, this unit can spawn up to five different types of pencil summons, including the resilient Pensman 3. It also features cloning capabilities with a maximum Red capacity of 55, allowing it to duplicate mid-tier units while outputting up to 11 million DPS.

### C Pen
Equipped with a razor and a minigun, the C Pen is a dedicated damage dealer. It requires a brief charge-up time before firing but compensates with high piercing damage. It is highly effective at shredding armored targets along straight pathways.

### Chief Pencil Guy
This hybrid unit summons Pencil Hammer Guys, C Pens, and standard Pencil Guys to the field. While its personal DPS is relatively low at 216,000, its strength lies in the sheer volume of summons it introduces to the lane.

### Blastery Colossal Pencor
The ultimate unit of the Pencil Race. Sporting an incredible 76 million max DPS, she is one of the strongest units in the game. Her high Red capacity of 245 allows her to clone powerful units, though she cannot clone giant units like the Upgraded Colossal Clock.

<Callout type="warning" title="Cost Warning">
Blastery Colossal Pencor is extremely expensive to deploy and upgrade. Avoid placing her too early in a match, as doing so can starve your economy and leave your lanes vulnerable to fast early-game waves.
</Callout>
