### Ultimate Camera Guy
The Ultimate Camera Guy is your primary choice for consistent, high-yield defensive setups. At lower levels, he relies on a gravity blast and spin attack. Once fully upgraded, his arsenal expands to include shoulder cannons, a gravity toss, and specialized camera turrets. The transition from four single-target turrets to three AOE-damaging turrets allows him to shred grouped enemy waves effortlessly.

### Ultimate Speaker Guy
With his distinct four-armed design, the Ultimate Speaker Guy excels at rapid-fire physical combat. His "Armada Help" passive is highly versatile, bringing in random support units that distract and damage enemies. At max level, he unlocks the Charged Arrow ability, which acts as a high-penetration core blast to clear dense lanes.

### Ultimate TV Duo
The Ultimate TV Duo represents the peak of sustained offensive power. With a high base placement cost of 13.64M, this unit is difficult to deploy but offers unmatched utility. The combination of Astro Speed (chain attacks) and life-steal stun healing makes them indispensable in ultra-late-game waves where enemy stun mechanics frequently disable your defenses.

### Ultimate Clock
The Ultimate Clock is the most expensive unit in the game, requiring a staggering 92.4M cash to place down. However, his utility is unmatched. His Enhanced Time Stop freezes the entire map for 60 seconds, during which the Ultimate Clock deals 1.5x more damage to all targets. Visually, he undergoes the most dramatic transformation, gaining floating clock gears, golden boots, and massive wings that unlock the Wing Shutter attack.

<Callout type="tip" title="Synergy Strategy">
Pair the Ultimate Clock with the Ultimate TV Duo. The TV Duo's stun-healing capability ensures your Ultimate Clock is never disabled, allowing you to cycle the 60-second Time Stop ability reliably during boss waves.
</Callout>
