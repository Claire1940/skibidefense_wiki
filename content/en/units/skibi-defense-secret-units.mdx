<Section icon={Target} title="How to Unlock Secret Units: Step-by-Step">

Unlocking **skibi defense secret units** isn't as simple as opening a crate. Most require specific actions within a match or reaching a certain milestone in the game's progression system. The process often involves "Secret Quests" that do not appear in your standard quest log until the first step is completed.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Reach Level 75+">
    Before most secret unit drops become active in the loot table, your account must reach a minimum level threshold. For the 2026 meta, Level 75 is the standard entry point for Secret-tier content.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Master Endless Mode">
    Many secret units, such as the Void Titan, only have a chance to drop after Wave 100 in Endless Mode. You must have a robust economy setup (using Farmers or similar units) to survive the late-game scaling.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Complete Hidden Map Objectives">
    Certain maps contain interactable objects. For example, on the "Abandoned City" map, clicking specific monitors in a certain order can trigger a mini-boss that drops the Shadow Operative fragment.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Collect Unit Fragments">
    Unlike standard units, secret units often require 10-50 fragments to craft. These fragments are rare drops from Nightmare difficulty bosses or high-wave Endless chests.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="The Crafting Ritual">
    Once fragments are collected, navigate to the "Secret Lab" in the main lobby. You will need a combination of fragments and a significant amount of in-game currency to finalize the unit.
  </Step>
</Steps>
